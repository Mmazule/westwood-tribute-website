Life Timeline

*1941- Born as Vivienne Isabel Swire in Derbyshire, England

*1958- She spent one term studying a jewelry and silversmith course at Harrow Art school (now University of Westminster)

*1961- Trained as an elementary school teacher

*1962- She married Derek Westwood in a wedding dress she made (later divorced)

*1963- Gave birth to her son, Benjamin

*1967- Had a son with her new partner, Malcolm McLaren

*1971- Started making clothes McLaren designed and opened the 'Let it Rock' shop. McLaren was the manager of the punk band, Sex Pistols. The band also wore McLaren's and Westwood's designs.
